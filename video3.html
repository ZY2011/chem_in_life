双氧水 + 醋 Hydrogen Peroxide + Vinegar

实验描述 Experiment Description

双氧水会冒泡，分解出氧气，能漂白、去污。加醋进去，它的分解会快一点，但有效成分也跑得快，等于白用。虽然不会马上出事，但效果差很多，所以最好别混着用，各干各的活。

Hydrogen peroxide fizzes and decomposes to release oxygen, which makes it effective for bleaching and stain removal. Adding vinegar will speed up its decomposition, causing the active ingredients to break down quickly—rendering it completely ineffective. While no immediate harm will follow, the product loses nearly all its efficacy, so it’s best to avoid mixing them and use each for its intended purpose alone.
