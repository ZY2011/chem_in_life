生活中的化学 Chem In Life

项目简介 Project Introduction

我们的项目旨在避免日常生活中不恰当地使用化学品，防止那些不起眼却可能发生的化学小事故， 通过手册的制作与分发来提升大家的安全意识，并鼓励积极参与预防行动。

Our project aims to prevent the inappropriate use of chemicals in daily life and to prevent seemingly insignificant but potentially dangerous chemical accidents. We raise safety awareness through the creation and distribution of manuals and encourage active participation in preventative measures.
