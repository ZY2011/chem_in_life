84消毒液 + 酒精 84 Disinfectant + Alcohol

实验描述 Experiment Description

84和酒精都是消毒用的，但混在一起会互相拆台。次氯酸钠会把酒精氧化掉，生成一非常刺激的气体(氯气或氯化氢)，还有可能刺激呼吸道。想要消毒就二选一，不要混用。

Both 84 disinfectant and alcohol are used for disinfection, but mixing them negates the effects of both. Sodium hypochlorite oxidizes alcohol, producing highly irritating gases (chlorine or hydrogen chloride), which may irritate the respiratory tract. Choose one for disinfection and avoid mixing them.
