高锰酸钾 + 酒精 Potassium Permanganate + Alcohol

实验描述 Experiment Description

这俩一碰，很危险。高锰酸钾是强氧化剂，酒精是易燃物，混一起会迅速发热，甚至烧起来。有人试过，结果桌子都着了。家里有小孩的尤其要注意，一定分开放，别让它们有机会见面.

Mixing these two is extremely dangerous. Potassium permanganate is a strong oxidizer, and alcohol is flammable. When combined, they generate heat rapidly and may even ignite. There have been cases where this mixture caused table fires. This is especially important to note for households with children: store them separately at all times to prevent accidental contact
