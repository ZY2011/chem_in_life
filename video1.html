84消毒液 + 洁厕灵 84消毒液 + 洁厕灵

实验描述 Experiment Description

这两种东西千万别一块儿倒进马桶里。84里有次氯酸钠，洁厕灵多是盐酸，一碰面就会冒出氯气。氯气味道特别呛，吸多了嗓子疼、胸闷，严重的还会伤肺。记住——这两瓶水永远分开用，间隔越久越好。

Never pour these two products into the toilet together.84 disinfectant contains sodium hypochlorite, and most toilet bowl cleaners are made with hydrochloric acid. When they mix, they release chlorine gas. Chlorine has a highly pungent odor; inhaling a large amount causes a sore throat and chest tightness, and severe exposure can damage the lungs. Remember—always use these two liquids separately, and the longer the interval between uses, the better.
