高锰酸钾 + 甘油 Potassium Permanganate + Vinegar

实验描述 Experiment Description

高锰酸钾是强氧化剂，甘油是常见有机物，两者一旦接触会迅速发热，甚至直接起火燃烧，有人实验中混合后瞬间引燃。千万别混放，尤其要远离孩子。

Potassium permanganate is a strong oxidizer, and glycerin is a common organic substance. Once they come into contact, they will generate heat rapidly and may even ignite and burn immediately. In some experiments, the mixture caught fire the moment they were combined. Never store them together, and keep them well away from children in particular.
